How is technology used

One of the solutions advocated by many was to use modern technology like the computer to improve student learning. But how we use the computer was not commonly considered until a popular nonprofit website came to the public's view.

It was called Khan academy

Khan academy is an online resource that offers learning lectures explaining many different subjects from math to computing to SAT.

  • The customization allows students to go at their own pace, with practices to enforce skills into memory and its online nature allows the learner to have easy access to it anywhere, even outside of the classroom.

The online aspect and internet based complication

  • If unable to connect to the internet, or loses internet, the learning can come to an halt

Many different subjects and classes options

  • The student may not know what they need to learn and can be overwhelmed by the options given.

Video lecture and practice problems

  • The lack of options beside only having video and practices may hinder students who need more help other than videos and practices.
