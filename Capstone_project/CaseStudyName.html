Traditionally, spending more time in school can improve the quality of the student's education

As a study showed that "Improved learning time program improved literarcy and math achievement when instruction was led by certified teacers, though the effect was small." Simply put. There are benefits to simply making students spend more time in the classroom that have shown small improvement for students taking those compared to students who don't.

Beside that, the study also showed that " Increased learning time improved the literacy achievement of students performing below standards and the social-emotional skills of students with attention deficit/ hyperactivity disorder." This mean that Increasing learning time helps students who are already below their peers and help them improve drastically compared to a normal student who also took more time learning.

How is technology used

One of the solutions advocated by many was to use modern technology like the computer to improve student learning. But how we use the computer was not commonly considered until a popular nonprofit website came to the public's view.

It was called Khan Academy

Benefits of the technology usage

  • Khan academy is an online resource that offers learning lectures explaining many different subjects from math to computing. It's customized dashboard allow for personalized education of your choice, being able to go back any time.The various subjects in Khan academy allows the user to be able to find things they wanted or needed to learn and help them practice on it. The customization allows students to go at their own pace, with practices to enforce skills into memory and its online nature allows the learner to have easy access to it anywhere, even outside of the classroom.

Implication 1: The online aspect

  • If unable to connect to the internet, or loses internet, the learning can come to an halt. As many places still don't have stable internet connection and eletronic to use Khan academy. The cost to maintain internet and pay to use computer may be difficult on tight budgets as not all schools can afford computers to use it.

Implication 2: Many different subjects and class options

  • The student may not know what they need to learn, as the student doesn't know what is the best for them. Too many options of different classes may overwhelm the user rather than helping them. The lack of teacher planning may harm the student's ability to learn a material.

Implication 3: Video lecture and practice problems

  • The lack of options beside only having video and practices may hinder students who need more help other than videos and practices. Not every students want to sit and watch lectures videos. There isn't other alternative as most of them are not fully face to face or hands on style of teaching.
